Our mission

Here at the Save Water organization,we intend to bring awareness to teenagers,children and youngsters about the necessity of water. We have focused on providing water resources to poor families and unprivileged villages of India. We conduct workshops and campaigns which are aimed to prevent contamination of water and conserve it. We have also started many hospitals for people who have fallen ill due to drinking contaminated water and water - related issues.
